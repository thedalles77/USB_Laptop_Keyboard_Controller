Glue_Beam is a stiffiner to glue at the middle seam for strength.
Kabel_holder adds a pinch piece to the USB cable holder.
Pads is same as original, just 6 times.
USB_Holeplugs is a add on to glue over the USB holes if not using them.
X61_Base_Left_Original is the left half of the original (still has Teensy 3.2 mounting holes)
X61_Base_right_Original is the right half of the original (still has Teensy 3.2 mountiing holes)
X61 Bezel_Left is the left half of the original bezel. I made no changes to this bezel.
X61 Bezel_right is the right half of the original bezel. I made no changes to this bezel.

I filled the mounting holes in both bezels with epoxy, then drilled small holes for short screws
but they still poked through. There's not much thickness to grab on to.
For future builds, install brass heat insert knurled nuts into the bezel mounting holes.
